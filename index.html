Unidentified Flying Objects, UFOs are the umbrella term for various aeriel phenomena that can not be explained. In some cases this means simply the eyewitnesses for different reasons were unable to identify a mundane, or natural object. This topic has created an industry of publications with various claims and levels of evidence. This site intends to attempt to put some organization on the cases, notable publications on the subject and what are the theories that can explain what has been observed.
